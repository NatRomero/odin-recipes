Burger Recipe

Home

Description

A classic American dish, burgers are the perfect food for a relaxed dinner or cookout. A juicy patty with meleted cheese in between two buns with whateevr toppings you desire. Burgers are delcious and even better when made at home!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • An egg
  • Choice of buns, I prefer Brioche
  • Cheddar Cheese Slices
  • Ice burg Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Optional: An Ice Cold Soda

Steps

  1. Begin by making lettuce slices then set aside for later
  2. In a bowl crack the egg then add the ground beef. Season to your liking, I enjoy salt, onion powder, and garlic powder.
  3. Mix the ground beef and egg till they are well blended together then make small balls about the size of an egg.
  4. Place the balls on a plate then use a bowl covered in parchment paper to squish the ball until it forms a patty.
  5. Place your patty onto a well heated grill or pan and cook on one side for about 3-4 minutes on a medium high heat.
  6. Flip the patty after the 4 minutes, let it cook for about 2 minutes then add the cheese.
  7. Remove the patty after an additional two minutes and place it on a bun.
  8. Add your desired toppings then enjoy with an ice cold soda.
